# AI Chatbot Usage Guide

## Quick Start

The AI chatbot is now available in both student and admin dashboards. Look for the floating chat button in the bottom-right corner of your screen.

## Accessing the Chatbot

### For Students:
1. Login to your account
2. Navigate to **Dashboard** (default view after login)
3. Look for the floating **green gradient button** with a message icon in the bottom-right corner
4. Click to open the chat window

### For Admins:
1. Login to your admin account
2. Navigate to **Admin Panel**
3. Look for the same floating button in the bottom-right corner
4. Click to open the chat window

## Using the Chatbot

### Opening the Chat:
- Click the floating button with the message icon
- The chat window will expand (400px wide × 600px tall)
- You'll see a personalized greeting based on the time of day

### Sending Messages:
1. Type your question in the input field at the bottom
2. Press **Enter** or click the **Send button** (paper plane icon)
3. Wait for the AI response (1-2 seconds)
4. View the response with optional suggestion chips

### Quick Actions:
- Click any **suggestion chip** to instantly send that query
- Suggestions are context-aware based on your role and data

### Window Controls:
- **Minimize**: Click the minimize icon to collapse to header only
- **Maximize**: Click again to expand back to full size
- **Close**: Click the X icon to close the chat completely
- **Reopen**: Click the floating button again to reopen

### Unread Messages:
- When minimized or closed, a **red badge** shows unread message count
- Badge disappears when you open the chat

## Troubleshooting

### Chatbot Not Appearing:
- Make sure you're on the Dashboard (students) or Admin Panel (admins)
- Check bottom-right corner of the screen
- Try refreshing the page

### Messages Not Sending:
- Ensure you've typed something in the input field
- Check your internet connection
- Try clicking Send button instead of pressing Enter

### Slow Responses:
- Normal delay is 1-2 seconds for realism
- If longer, check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page

### Chat Window Too Small/Large:
- Window is fixed at 400px × 600px
- Use minimize feature if it's in the way
- Close completely if not needed

## Privacy & Data

### What the Chatbot Knows:
- Your name and user ID
- Your role (student or admin)
- Your quiz score (students only)
- Your weak topics (students only)
- Your recent activity status

### What It Doesn't Know:
- Your password
- Other users' private data (unless you're admin)
- Information outside the platform
- Your browsing history

### Data Storage:
- Chat history is stored in session (temporary)
- Cleared when you logout or close browser
- Not saved to database (currently)
- Future: Optional chat history persistence
